KOINONIA 8.0 - Directions to St Arnold Jassen Spirituality Center

1. Directions from Accra (Central/ East/North) ▼
  • The Route: Join the main Mallam-Kasoa Highway (N1) heading towards Central Region/Kasoa.
  • Key Milestones: Pass through McCarthy Hill, Weija, and cross the Weija Lake bridge.
  • Where to Branch: Just after crossing the bridge and passing the site of the Old Toll Booth, look out for Tuba Junction.
  • Final Stretch: Branch left at Tuba Junction onto the Tuba-Kokrobite road. Follow the main township road heading up towards the scenic Tuba Hill. Ask for or follow signs to the St. Arnold Janssen Spirituality Centre (AJSC).
2. Directions from Winneba (and the Central/Western Region) ▼
  • The Route: Take the main Winneba-Accra Road (N1) heading towards Accra.
  • Key Milestones: Pass through Budumburam and drive straight through the main Kasoa township.
  • Where to Branch: Keep driving past Kasoa towards Accra. Before you reach the Weija Lake bridge, you will arrive at Tuba Junction (just before the old toll booth site).
  • Final Stretch: Turn right at Tuba Junction. Follow the road into the township and head up towards the Tuba Hill area where the spirituality centre is located.
3. Directions from Kumasi (and the Ashanti/Eastern Region) ▼
  • The Route: Take the main Kumasi-Accra Highway (N6) down south.
  • Connecting Route (To Avoid Central Accra Traffic): When you approach the outskirts of Accra, it is best to take the Nsawam-Amasaman-Pokuase route or use the Akweteman/Lapaz connection onto the George Walker Bush Highway (N1) heading toward Mallam.
  • Key Milestones: From the Mallam Interchange, drive straight on the Mallam-Kasoa Highway past Weija.
  • Where to Branch: Right after passing the Old Toll Booth area, look for Tuba Junction.
  • Final Stretch: Branch left at the junction and follow the road up into the Tuba Hill community to locate the centre.
